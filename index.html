Technology


Blockchain

Blockchain technology is built to be tamper-proof, which is uniquely suitable for record keeping in food and medicine production industries. We use blockchain as the fundamental technology in our solution to establish a new framework of certified provenance standard so that cultivators can show their chain of custody while demonstrating exceptional quality.

Smart Logistics

We integrate logistic solutions into our blockchain services. Our pioneering experience in smart logistics gives us a unique ability to lead smart technologies design and deployment in the logistics field.

IoT Management Platform
